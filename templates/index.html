About Site

The PG Booking Management System is designed to simplify and automate the process of booking PG and hostel rooms. Students can browse available properties, book rooms, track booking approvals, check-in status, and make payments securely.

On the other side, Property Owners can manage their PG listings, approve booking requests, monitor room occupancy, and review payment history from students.
